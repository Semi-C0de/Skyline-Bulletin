Cross Country Person

Skyline High School’s Anna Callahan captured 1st place as an individual in the 4A girls championship race, and the Spartan girls placed 7th as a team!

Last week, King 5 News featured XC runners Anna Callahan and Rebecca O Keefe. These are two amazing athletes. Anna has run a 16:43 5km during the season which is the fastest time ever by a Washington Female and Rebecca has run a 17:07 which is number 6 all time.
